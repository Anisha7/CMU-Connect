Research

PROBLEM

All online resources needed by a college student are very cluttered among different websites. This adds additional time-consuming work to the already over-busy schedules of students. Currently, the platforms in use by students and faculty in my university are SIO, Canvas, Gradescope, and Autolab. SIO is the university's official site, which handles finances and course lookup/registration. The other sites are used by different classes to handle grading and online submissions. Each student ends up using a variety of these sources for their classes. It becomes a hassle to keep track of each and every source.
